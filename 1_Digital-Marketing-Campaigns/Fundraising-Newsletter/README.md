# Fundraising Newsletter Campaign

## Overview
The Fundraising Newsletter was a core revenue-driving communication designed to engage donors, highlight impactful stories, and encourage continued financial support. I led this project end-to-end, transforming a traditionally print-first asset into a fully integrated digital campaign.

## My Role
- Campaign lead from concept to execution  
- Copywriting, storytelling, message framing  
- Visual design and layout direction  
- Print production coordination  
- Data cleansing, segmentation & mailing list preparation  
- Digital transformation to website banners, email content, and social media assets  

## Strategy & Approach
The strategy centered on emotionally compelling storytelling paired with clear calls to action. I ensured consistency across all communication channels, optimizing donor engagement and reinforcing ARC’s mission.

## Execution
- Designed newsletter layout and content flow  
- Managed print vendor briefing, proofing, revisions, and final production  
- Built optimized donor segments for targeted outreach  
- Created website tiles, banners, and icons  
- Developed social media creatives to extend reach  
- Adapted content for email newsletters  

## Impact & Outcomes
- Strengthened ARC’s donor engagement and brand trust  
- Increased visibility and consistency across all digital platforms  
- Enabled more streamlined donor communication processes
