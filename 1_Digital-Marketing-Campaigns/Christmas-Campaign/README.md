# Christmas Direct Mail Campaign

## Overview
One of ARC’s most important annual fundraising initiatives, combining direct mail, storytelling, and digital amplification.

## My Role
- Led the full DM workflow (concept → design → printing → distribution)  
- Managed donor segmentation & data processing  
- Converted print assets into digital formats  
- Produced social media and website updates  

## Strategy & Approach
Tell an emotionally compelling holiday story that builds empathy and motivates giving, supported by cohesive branding across all channels.

## Execution
- Developed DM letters, envelopes, inserts  
- Worked with print vendors & managed timelines  
- Created web banners, email headers & social assets  
- Ensured accurate donor list segmentation  

## Impact & Outcomes
- Strengthened donor relationships  
- Supported year-end fundraising targets  
- Improved storytelling consistency across all ARC communications
