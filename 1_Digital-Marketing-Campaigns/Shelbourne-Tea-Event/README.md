# Shelbourne Tea Event Campaign

## Overview
A high-profile fundraising and awareness event hosted in partnership with The Shelbourne. ARC required cohesive digital and print promotion to increase attendance and community engagement.

## My Role
- Led creative and messaging direction  
- Produced print & digital promotional assets  
- Supported event-day communications  
- Coordinated with internal teams and event partners  

## Strategy & Approach
Position the event as an elegant, meaningful, community-driven experience. Messaging focused on connection, support, and shared purpose.

## Execution
- Designed event posters, invitations, and leaflets  
- Built social media promotional content  
- Provided real-time coverage support  
- Ensured visual consistency across all materials  

## Impact & Outcomes
- Enhanced event visibility  
- Strengthened donor and partner relationships  
- Supported a positive brand experience
