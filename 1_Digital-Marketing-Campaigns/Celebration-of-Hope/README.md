# Celebration of Hope Campaign

## Overview
A signature ARC event celebrating survivorship, resilience, and community. This campaign required emotionally resonant messaging and multi-channel visibility.

## My Role
- Creative lead for the event’s full communication suite  
- Copywriting, design support & campaign rollout  
- Digital asset development for website & social  

## Strategy & Approach
Blend hope-driven storytelling with powerful visuals to inspire community participation.

## Execution
- Designed event collateral (flyers, posters, online banners)  
- Produced social media content during the event cycle  
- Updated website sections to reflect event branding  

## Impact & Outcomes
- Increased community participation  
- Reinforced ARC’s identity as a compassionate, supportive organisation
