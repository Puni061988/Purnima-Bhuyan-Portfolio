# Services Newsletter Campaign

## Overview
The Services Newsletter highlighted ARC’s holistic cancer support offerings, helping service users understand available programmes while strengthening ARC’s brand presence.

## My Role
- Sole owner of newsletter content, structure, and creative  
- Copywriting based on service team insights  
- Design direction and print asset creation  
- Data preparation for distribution  
- Conversion to digital formats for website & social media  

## Strategy & Approach
The goal was to create an informative, uplifting, and user-focused narrative. The content strategy ensured that service users, families, and partners clearly understood ARC’s range of supports.

## Execution
- Drafted newsletter stories, imagery, and layout  
- Coordinated printing and distribution  
- Created digital tiles & banners for the ARC website  
- Designed social posts to increase programme uptake  

## Impact & Outcomes
- Improved service awareness among users  
- Strengthened consistency in ARC’s brand communication  
- Supported service teams through improved programme visibility
